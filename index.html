Göteborg Vegamap

A map of vegetarian and vegan-friendly restaurants/cafés/bars

Help

  • Click and drag the map to navigate around Göteborg
  • Use the mouse scroll wheel or pinch the screen to zoom in/out
  • Click on a location's icon to get more information
  • Select tags on the bottom of the screen to filter locations
